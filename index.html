This is About Us!

Our mission at Lemon It Up is to deliver fresh and sweet tea to our customers. We bring friends and family together through our delightful and tasty lemonade. We are an easy-going business that serves their customers the best lemon tea they could ever have. We offer a wide range of tea, but we are most known for our homemade lemon tea

Lemon Tea

Enjoy all the benefits of drinking our homemade tea! It's made with our secret recipe. We also sell custom tea bags for our customers who may enjoy other flavors such as raspberry or blueberry tea.

Iced Raspberry Tea

Our raspberry tea made with our custom tea bags, fresh raspberries, and lemonade. It has a subtle, refreshing yet sweet taste. We can also customize the sugar to your liking and offer sugar-free options.

Honey Tea

With our honey tea, we can do a blend of different ingredients to your liking. We offer blueberry honey tea, raspberry honey tea, or lemon honey tea. If you have a custom order in mind, look out for us in the future; we are developing this!
